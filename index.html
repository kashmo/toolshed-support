ToolWise

Know what you own before you buy another one.

A native iPhone app for homeowners. Catalog your tools once — then answer "do I already own this?" in seconds, whether you're standing in the store aisle, at a job site, or in the garage.

Highlights

  • ◆
    Three fast ways to add a tool Scan a barcode, speak the tool name aloud, or type it in — whichever fits the moment.
  • ◆
    UPC lookup baked in Scanning fetches the product name, brand, model, category, and even the product image automatically.
  • ◆
    Wishlist for tools you're considering Not ready to buy? Save it for later. When you actually buy it, one tap moves it to your inventory.
  • ◆
    Battery + package tracking Track cordless battery platforms (Milwaukee M18, DeWalt 20V, Stihl AK, EGO 56V, and many more) and package sizes for bulk fasteners.
  • ◆
    Custom brands and locations Add brands and locations the app doesn't already know about. Custom entries persist and appear in "Recent" for one-tap access.
  • ◆
    iCloud sync Your inventory (and photos) follow you across every Apple device signed into your iCloud account.
  • ◆
    iPhone only, iOS 17+ Built native. No accounts, no subscriptions, no data sold.

Currently in Beta

ToolWise is in closed TestFlight testing with a small group of friends and family. Public release will follow once the feedback loop wraps up. If you were invited to test, thank you — your feedback is shaping every build.
